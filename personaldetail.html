Get to know Julia on a personal level!

Through Saturday Japanese school for 14 years, I learned Japanese while going to American school like every other kid. I played basketball growing up and ran track and field for a couple years as well! I am interested in health and fitness and this comes hand-in-hand with my love for learning about nutrition!
